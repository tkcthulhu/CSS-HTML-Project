My Destructive Hobby Habit

So I have a belief that every one needs 3 hobbies:

  • One that keeps you healthy
  • One that keeps you creative
  • One that keeps your mind sharp

This is all very well and good, but there's a problem, having three hobbies doesn't seem like it would be that big of a deal right? However I now introduce you to the concept of...*drumroll*

The Sub-Hobby.

Let me explain...

Whenever you have a hobby, there are always going to be extra things that you have to do in order to make the most of your investment in sed hobby, or things that you do in order to make it ever more enjoyable. My personal hobbies are playing guitar, olympic weightlifting, and playing chess these are hobbies that I feel like meet all of those criteria and things that I do enjoy, but they dont really leave a lot of time for much else. These are also only my main hobbies, not even inculding all the other things I have picked up throughout the years... ADHD is a hell of a drug guys.
